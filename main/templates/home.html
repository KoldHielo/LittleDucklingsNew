Meet Laura

Professional care with a calm, family-first feel.

Hello, I'm Laura and I'm the proud owner of Little Ducklings Childminding in Baddeley Green, Stoke-on-Trent. I live here with my husband Kieran and our two children, and I've created the kind of setting that helps little ones feel at ease from the moment they arrive.

I believe a home environment can make the transition into childcare feel gentler, warmer, and more reassuring. Children are welcomed into a space where they can play, settle, and grow in confidence while being cared for as individuals.

I would love to welcome you and your little one for a visit so you can get a feel for daily life at Little Ducklings yourself.

Approach

Focusing on confidence, curiosity, and everyday wellbeing.

Whilst having lots of fun through play, I carefully plan activities that support each child's learning and development. Their interests and needs stay at the centre of everything we do.

Free-flow play, warm relationships, and consistent encouragement help children feel happy, safe, and ready to discover new things.
