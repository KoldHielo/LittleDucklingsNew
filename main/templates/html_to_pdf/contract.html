Policies & Information

Early Arrivals / Late Collections

Early arrivals will need to be arranged with the childminder and charged at £1.50 per 15 minutes. Late collections will also be charged at £1.50 per 15 minutes.

Holidays

Parents on holiday during times when the childminder is available will still be required to pay normal fees. The childminder’s own holidays will not incur fees.

Illness

If a child is unable to attend due to illness, original fees will still be charged. If the childminder is ill, no fees will be charged for this period.

Bank Holidays & Training Days

The childminder will not be open on bank holidays. Training days may be arranged on non-working days; parents will not be charged for these.

Payments

Fees are payable 4-weekly in advance. Accepted methods: Bank Transfer (BACS) or cash. Late payments incur a £5/day penalty unless agreed. Contract termination may occur if fees remain unpaid.

Settling In Agreement

Parents and childminder can arrange a settling-in period. Sessions must be paid at the normal rate. The childminder may extend this period if needed for the child’s welfare.

Contract Termination

One month’s notice in writing is required for termination by either parent or childminder. All fees must be paid in full before contract termination.
